## Sentence Summary

This DIP activates Advisory Council governance and formally initiates the first governance epoch.

## Para Summaries

This proposal establishes the formal commencement of protocol governance by the Advisory Council, transitioning authority from interim Company-led governance to structured, auditable, and rule-based Council governance in accordance with the Constitution and Policy.

## Modules Summary

**DIP0m1: Governance Activation**  
Defines the triggers for governance activation.

**DIP0m2: Transition of Authority**  
Describes interim governance cessation and protocol governance transfer.

**DIP0m3: Governance Epoch**  
Implements the activation date for the first governance epoch.

**DIP0m4: Regulatory & Legal Considerations**  
Describes Governance Authority & ensures separation between Corporate & Protocol governance.

**DIP0m5: Risk Considerations**  
Governance ambiguity risk mitigated via formal activation trigger.


## Motivation

The Constitution establishes governance authority but requires formal activation to become operational. A clearly defined transition ensures:

* Legal certainty
* Governance legitimacy
* Auditability of decision-making
* Regulatory clarity regarding control and responsibility


## Specification / Proposal Details

### DIP0m1: Governance Activation Conditions

Governance shall be deemed operational only upon:

* Formal appointment of all seven (7) Council Members
* Confirmation of governance commencement date by the Company

---

### DIP0m2: Transition of Authority

* Interim governance (Article IC) shall cease upon activation 
* Protocol governance authority transfers to the Advisory Council
* Corporate authority remains reserved to the Company (Article IA)

---

### DIP0m3: Governance Epoch

* The first governance epoch shall begin on the activation date
* All protocol-level decisions must thereafter be executed through approved DIPs

---

### DIP0m4: Regulatory & Legal Considerations

This DIP does not constitute:

* A financial promotion
* Investment advice
* An offer of securities
* Governance authority remains **protocol-bound only** and does not override Company legal duties
* Ensures clear separation between: Corporate governance & Protocol governance (Article XVI)

---

### DIP0m5: Risk Considerations

* Risk of premature activation → mitigated by requiring full Council formation
* Risk of governance ambiguity → mitigated via formal activation trigger
